Card Memory Game

How to Play:
  • Click on any two cards to flip them over.
  • If the two cards match, they stay face up and count towards your score.
  • If they don't match, they flip back down after a short delay.
  • Try to remember card positions to find pairs faster.
  • You have 60 seconds to find all matching pairs before time runs out.
  • The game ends when all pairs are found or time expires.
  • Your goal is to find all pairs in the fewest moves possible.
  • Use the speaker button to mute or unmute game sounds.
